LASY Marketing OS

Marketing automation platform for D2C beauty brands

About

LASY Marketing OS is a marketing automation platform that helps D2C (direct-to-consumer) beauty brands manage their content posting and performance analytics across connected social platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Google Analytics, Coupang, Naver Commerce). Brand operators authorize their own platform accounts via OAuth, then use our system to schedule pre-approved content, monitor engagement metrics, and generate daily performance reports through an AI pipeline.

How it works

  • 1. Connect — Brand operators sign in and authorize their brand's social media accounts via OAuth (TikTok Login Kit, Instagram, etc.)
  • 2. Create — AI generates draft content based on brand guidelines; operators review and approve
  • 3. Publish — Approved content is sent to the brand's inbox (or directly published if opted in)
  • 4. Analyze — Daily Cron retrieves engagement metrics; AI generates next-day content recommendations
