Privacy Policy — LASY Marketing OS

Last updated: May 9, 2026
Effective date: May 9, 2026

LASY Marketing OS ("we", "us", "our"), operated by SY COSMETICS CO., LTD., is a marketing automation platform ("Service") that enables D2C beauty brands to manage content posting and performance analytics across connected social platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Google Analytics, Coupang, Naver Commerce). Brand operators authorize their own platform accounts via OAuth, and our system processes data only for the connected, authorized brand accounts.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect the information we access through the TikTok API and other integrated platforms on behalf of brands that connect to our Service. We process data only for accounts that brand operators have explicitly authorized our app to access.

1. Information We Collect from TikTok

Through the TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API, with explicit authorization from a connecting brand's TikTok business account, we access and store:

  • Account identifiers: open_id of the authorizing brand account
  • Video metadata: video id, title, create_time, duration
  • Performance metrics: view_count, like_count, comment_count, share_count
  • Authentication tokens: access_token and refresh_token, used solely to call TikTok APIs on behalf of the authorizing brand account

We do not access:

  • Private messages or inbox content
  • Personal data of other TikTok users (followers, viewers, commenters) beyond what is publicly visible in engagement on the brand's own posts
  • Videos or data from any account other than the one that explicitly authorized our app

2. How We Use the Information

  • Performance analysis: aggregating daily video metrics to generate per-brand marketing reports
  • Content recommendation: feeding aggregated metrics into our AI pipeline (Anthropic Claude) to recommend next-day content strategy
  • Content publishing: if the video.publish scope is authorized, publishing approved content on behalf of the brand account

We do not sell, rent, or share TikTok-derived data with third parties for advertising or profiling purposes. Each brand's data is isolated from other brands on the platform.

3. Third-Party Processing

Aggregated performance metrics (numeric summaries, not raw user data) may be sent to:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — for AI-generated daily briefings and content recommendations. Only aggregated numbers and brand-side text are sent; no TikTok user personal data is transmitted.
  • Google BigQuery (asia-northeast3) — for storage and analytics

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Location: Google Cloud Platform, BigQuery, region asia-northeast3 (Seoul, Republic of Korea)
  • Access tokens: stored encrypted in our credentials store. access_token is refreshed every 20 hours; old tokens are discarded.
  • Refresh tokens: stored encrypted for up to 365 days. Upon revocation or expiration, they are deleted within 24 hours.
  • Performance data: retained for up to 24 months to enable year-over-year analysis, then deleted.
  • Immediate deletion: upon app uninstall, authorization revocation, or account closure request, all associated tokens and derived personal identifiers are deleted within 72 hours.
  • Per-brand isolation: each connected brand's data is logically isolated; one brand cannot access another brand's data.

5. Your Rights

  • Revoke access: you may revoke our app's access at any time from TikTok Settings → Security → Manage app permissions.
  • Request data deletion: email us at the address below, and we will delete all data associated with your authorization within 30 days.
  • Access your data: you may request a copy of the data we hold linked to your TikTok authorization.

6. Security

We protect stored data using industry-standard measures:

  • All tokens encrypted at rest (Google KMS) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Access limited to authorized platform personnel via SSO and MFA
  • BigQuery access controlled via IAM roles with least-privilege principle
  • Per-brand data isolation enforced at query level
  • Regular security audits

7. Compliance with TikTok Platform Policies

We use TikTok's APIs in compliance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. We do not store TikTok content beyond what is necessary to provide our Service. We do not attempt to reverse-engineer, scrape, or bypass any technical protections of the TikTok platform.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for use by authorized brand operators only and is not directed to individuals under the age of 14. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International Data Transfer

Data is stored in South Korea (asia-northeast3). If data is accessed from other countries by our authorized personnel, it remains subject to this Privacy Policy and applicable Korean data protection laws (PIPA, Personal Information Protection Act).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Material changes will be notified through our support channel.
